Tony & Cleo
Adopted


 have spent their lives together and would like to continue living together. Do you have room in your life for two sweet rabbits?

Cleo is the spayed female, approximately 2 years old in Sept. 1996. She is a Jersey Woolly, overweight and needs to be put on a strict diet. Cleo will need to be brushed a minimum of once each week and more when she begins shedding. Angora’s are bred to shed every three months and we recommend that you give her a haircut every two months to attempt to avoid hairballs.

Tony is the male, also 2 years old in September 1996. He is a beautiful brown brindle color and is also overweight. Tony was neutered on 5/11/96 at the Bellevue Humane Society.
