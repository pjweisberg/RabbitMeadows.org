HRS MEMBERSHIP DUES INCREASE FOR THE FIRST TIME, EVER

HRS has increased the dual membership fees for the combined national and local membership to $25.00 (from $22.00). It is the first increase in fees in the history of the Washington HRS. Each dollar will continue to work just as hard in rescuing and   caring for rabbits.
