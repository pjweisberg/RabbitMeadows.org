Youth House Rabbit Club members were challenged to add their own endings to the House Rabbit Society's Easter Bunny Poem.  Here are the results below.  The winner is revealed in the Hall of Fame!

Entry B

Until one rueful, colorless day,
I sat despairing, sadly staring
as someone opened the door
Of my cage.


I lifted my head out of this poor, deserted place,
As I peered into a small girl's brightly hopeful face.
Then suddenly, my spirits rose, hoping I  soon would leave
This dusty, dirty, cage.

I stood staight and tall, bright and proud,
at being given a second chance.
I pointed my ears toward the ceiling,and hoped to look appealing
As she lifted me out
of my cage

She affectionatly smoothed my tangled fur,
But then her father scolded her:
"Put it back, we don't want that old, dirty animal!"
But she refused to put me in my cage.

The memories of my first life flooded back, and a tear wound a track
Down my quivering face.
I didn't want to go thru it again, I'd rather stay here,
In my cage.

But, her warm touch and gentle smile
Made trusting her worth while.
I somehow knew that this time would be different,
In my new cage.

And though I knew they would be my last,
These new days could replace the past.
I left in peace, happier than ever, as my companion, my friend, sat beside
My cage.

Entry C

Barking dogs and mewing cats
Everywhere an abandoned pet is dying
Hungry and sick I just sit crying
In my cage

There must be a chance somewhere
Humans come to look and see
I remember long ago when humans loved me
In my cage

A woman comes who really likes me
She says she is a vet
She brings me home, now I'm her pet
In my cage

She lets me out that day
I sniff my surroundings cautiously
This is where I want to be
Out of my cage

Then comes the sunny afternoon
I meet a rabbit by the pond
That very day we happily bond
Out of my cage

And we live together every day
A lucky star I have found
Everything around me is always safe and sound
Out of my cage

Entry A

I'm thankful for the cuddles Maggie gives me.
I'm thankful for Maggie's cute adorable face waiting for pets when I have to
get up at some awful early hour.
I'm thankful for Maggie's long hair that always needs brushing, for a place
to just sit and be calm.
I'm thankful for Maggie's silly uppy downy ears that always make me laugh.
I'm thankful for Willow's bites, because they teach me to deal with the

I'm thankful Willow hated my psychology book, because it showed me I don't
want to major in psychology.  By the way, he doesn't hate my Guerilla Girls

I'm thankful for Willow's ever destruction of magazines, since most of them
are dumb anyways.

Entry B

    I am thankful for my rabbit ''Speckels'' because she is my good luck
bunny, whenever I have a test at school I always do good if she gives me
kisses in the morning. I am thankful to have her, because she is my third bun
and the other two had died within the first two weeks.  I am thankful she is so
social because we have five other cats she has to put up with.  I am also
thankful she loves me as much as she does considering my brother and friends
like to play rough with her. I am thankful she is so smart or she would get
herself into alot of trouble! I am thankful to see her every morning with not
only a wake-up call, but looking at me with happiness and gratitude.
She puts up with alot and I love her from the tip of her puffy black tail to
the end of her twitching black nose with the single white spot that drives me crazy! -Kristina

Entry C

I am thankful for all the bunny kisses and love,

I am certain that Little Bun was sent from above.

I am thankful for all the times that we share,

And how you always seem to care.

I am thankful for all the many wonderful days,

You have shown me love in so many ways.

I am thankful for the times we cuddle up close,

And then you know who I love the most.

I am thankful for how you are so close to my heart,

I have always loved you, even right from the start.

I am thankful for how you are always around,

You pick me up when I am feeling down.

I am thankful for how you calm all my fears,

You are always there to wipe away my tears.

Little Bun, I am thankful for all you are,

You truly are my bright and shining star.

 -Jennifer
