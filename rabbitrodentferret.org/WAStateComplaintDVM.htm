Veterinarian euthanized Foxy without our permission or knowledge!

Dr. Cathy Johnson-Delaney killed Foxy on Nov. 1, 2003 at the veterinary clinic she has established at Washington Ferret Association (WFA) a nonprofit org. In Washington state a veterinary clinic run by a non-profit animal shelter may ONLY be used for spay/neuters. (This clinic is used primarily for descenting, tumor surgeries, teeth cleaning, vascetomies and many other procedures. Most ferrets received by all shelters are already spayed/neutered, so there are very few spay/neuter surgeries necessary. In addition to Dr. Johnson-Delaney's horrible behavior regarding Foxy's euthanazation, this clinic violates Washington State law RCW 18.92.260.)

Foxy (and Sally) went to Dr. Johnson-Delaney for her monthly lupron injection. Instead, Foxy was killed (Sally was returned. Thankfully.) Dr. Johnson-Delaney now refuses to return Foxy's body to us. Apparently Dr. Johnson-Delaney considers Foxy's body evidence of her murder, otherwise why would she be keeping her from us?

We can not bring Foxy back. She is gone. But the only way we are going to find any peace is to have Foxy returned home.

We have filed a complaint with the Washington State Veterinary Board. http://www.RabbitRodentFerret.org/Complaint.htm
