   Rabbits often enjoy a good x-mas tree. To eat ... not to look at. Keep flocked trees away from your rabbit. As with this tree, lights & decorations must be kept above bunny height when he's on tip toes. Or, you can use an x-pen to enclose the tree to keep bunnies at a safe distance from the tree.

    If you want to enjoy some time under the tree with your bunny, you can hang baby carrots and other veggies from the bottom of your tree using ornament hangers. Your bunny will enjoy the healthy treat and it's fun for both of you.
