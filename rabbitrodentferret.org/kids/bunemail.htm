BUN-E-MAIL

Bun-E-Mail is the Youth House Rabbit Club members' discussion list. 

Not a member yet?  Click here

FOR: kids, teens and young adults who are interested in rabbits as indoor companion animals -- house pets.

PURPOSE: The purpose of this list is to provide a place where young rabbit lovers can correspond with one another; make friends, share experiences with our rabbits, ask and answer questions.  Are you a youth who would like to discuss house rabbits?  Subscribe today!

The main topic of this list is house rabbits, and as much as possible we like to stay on-topic.  Both rabbit and non-rabbit chatter is allowed in the chat room (only subscribers are able to use the bunemail egroups chat room). Subscribers who have rabbits for purposes other than the one mentioned above must keep in mind the topic of our list when posting.  If you post on what isn't allowed, you may be moderated.

WHAT ISN'T ALLOWED: Forwarding chain letters, jokes or virus warnings.  Offensive postings, foul or inappropriate language.  Promoting rabbits as outdoor animals, discussing breeding or rabbits as meat.  Please go to "what we have decided not to discuss and why" to read why particular topics should not be discussed.

What We Have Decided Not to Discuss and Why

BREEDING:  Why it's not allowed

1. This club, for young people interested in rabbits as house pets, is not about breeding.  See About the Club.

2. This topic has caused problems on the list before.

3. There are so many adoptable rabbits (click here for examples!) out there waiting for good homes.  Breeding adds to the overpopulation.  Even if you find good homes for all your rabbits, meanwhile, rabbits are waiting or being euthanized in shelters due to lack of a good home. 

4. If you feel that you are a responsible breeder and still should be able to discuss breeding, you are certainly entitled to -- on a list that includes breeding in its topics! BEM doesn't.

LIVESTOCK:  Why it's not allowed

This is a house rabbit club for young people.  Members keep rabbits as companion animals.
