For the love of Rabbits, Rodents and Ferrets

This scholarship fund is established in memory of Dr. Barbara Deeb.

She was a compassionate veterinarian to her patients and clients alike. From 1990 until her death she volunteered her time to help rabbits, rodents & ferrets at House Rabbit Society, Rabbit Meadows Sanctuary & Best Little Rabbit, Rodent & Ferret House. She freely gave her time to educate caregivers of these small animals at numerous workshops & was a presenter at the first House Rabbit Society Veterinary Conference. She spent long hours on the phone in consultation with other veterinarians across the country.

We hope that recipients of this scholarship will follow in her footsteps, keeping her memory, her passion and her compassion alive.
