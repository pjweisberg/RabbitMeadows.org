I have tried to write Rabbit Health 101 for the average rabbit caretaker — one who has little or no medical background. This book is not intended to ever take the place of a visit to a veterinarian — if anything, it should increase your contact with your veterinarian. It is intended to give you, as a caretaker, the information you need to play an active and informed role in your rabbit’s medical care.
