Volunteer!  (Did someone say pizza?) As you can see above, we're clearing the area where our new shelter will be built. Building should commence by mid-May! We need your help. Before construction, we need help removing debris from the site to a pile where it can be turned into chips, which can then be used for landscaping material. There are some small fir trees that need to be dug up and potted until they can be replanted, and many more small jobs. Join us at our Redmond location this Sunday, 4/17/11 between 10 & dark. Pizza break in the afternoon.(Questions: 425-836-8925)
